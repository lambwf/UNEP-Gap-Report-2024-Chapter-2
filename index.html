Data sources

The following underlying sources are used to produce all figures:

  • The EDGAR v8.0 database of greenhouse gas emissions (Crippa et al. 2024)
  • The Global Carbon Budget database of historic CO2 emissions (Friedlingstein et al. 2023)
  • The National GHG Inventory Database of CO2 LULUCF emissions (Grassi et al. 2024)
  • The UNFCCC National Inventory Submissions (UNFCCC 2024)

Emissions are grouped into common sectors based on this scheme. We use global warming potentials with a time horizon of 100 years (GWP100) from the IPCC AR6 to aggregate greenhouse gas emissions.
